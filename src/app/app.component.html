Conclusion

  1. Standup total time grows exponentially, add participants carefully.
  2. Splitting standups above certain team size is a good way to reduce total time.
  3. In reality people tend to speak longer if there are less participants.
  4. A standup with more participants can be used to share the big picture. The risk is that not everyone listens to everyone.
  5. A standup with less participants can be used to share details. The risk is that standup becomes a discussion.
